NetSuite-Salesforce Enterprise Integration

Bi-directional data synchronization between Oracle NetSuite ERP and Salesforce CRM via Celigo iPaaS, delivering unified customer views, automated order processing, and real-time operational visibility.

99.5% Data Accuracy Target

The Problem

When sales teams work in Salesforce while finance and operations live in NetSuite, the gap between systems creates friction at every step of the customer lifecycle.

The challenges compound quickly:

  • Disconnected systems — Sales can't see inventory levels, order status, or invoice history from NetSuite
  • Double data entry — Customer information entered separately in both systems, inviting errors
  • Order processing delays — Closed deals require manual re-entry into NetSuite before fulfillment begins
  • No fulfillment visibility — Sales calls operations to check shipping status instead of seeing it directly
  • Fragmented customer view — Finance and sales see different versions of customer data
  • Compliance gaps — No unified audit trail across systems for reporting and reconciliation

The Solution

A comprehensive integration connecting NetSuite and Salesforce through Celigo's iPaaS platform, enabling seamless data flow in both directions with intelligent error handling and complete operational visibility.

Core Capabilities

  • Bi-Directional Customer Synchronization — Customer and account data flows seamlessly between systems, keeping both platforms current
  • Automated Order Creation — Closed Won opportunities in Salesforce automatically become Sales Orders in NetSuite
  • Product Catalog Alignment — NetSuite items and pricing reflected in Salesforce for accurate quoting
  • Real-Time Order Tracking — Fulfillment status and shipping details flow back to Salesforce automatically
  • Invoice Visibility — Finance-posted invoices visible in Salesforce for sales follow-up and collections support
  • Intelligent Error Handling — Automated retry logic and error queuing ensure data integrity
  • Complete Audit Trail — Every synchronization logged and traceable for compliance

Architecture

The integration uses Celigo's iPaaS platform as the central orchestration layer, connecting Salesforce and NetSuite through purpose-built integration flows that handle data transformation, error recovery, and audit logging.

Technical Highlights

Unified Customer Master

Customer data remains consistent across sales and finance operations. Changes in either system propagate automatically, eliminating the "which system is correct?" question that plagues disconnected environments.

  • Address changes sync within minutes
  • Credit terms and payment history visible to sales
  • Contact relationships maintained across both systems

Accelerated Quote-to-Cash

When opportunities close in Salesforce, orders appear in NetSuite automatically. No manual handoff, no re-keying, no delays.

  • Opportunity line items become Sales Order lines
  • Pricing and discounts preserved accurately
  • Warehouse receives orders faster

Operational Visibility Without System Access

Sales teams see order status, shipping information, and invoice details directly in Salesforce — without needing NetSuite access or calling operations.

  • Tracking numbers and ship dates visible in CRM
  • Invoice amounts and due dates for collections support
  • Customer inquiries answered without system hopping

Resilient Architecture

Built to handle real-world conditions: network issues, API limits, and temporary outages don't cause data loss.

  • Automatic retry on transient failures
  • Error queue for manual review when needed
  • No duplicate records from retry scenarios

Business Application

Here's how the integration supports a typical quote-to-cash workflow:

  1. Sales rep creates opportunity in Salesforce with line items and pricing
  2. Customer updates their address in NetSuite — change syncs to Salesforce automatically
  3. Sales rep closes the deal (Opportunity → Closed Won)
  4. Sales Order created in NetSuite within 2 minutes — no manual entry
  5. Warehouse picks, packs, and ships — creates Item Fulfillment
  6. Tracking number and ship date appear in Salesforce within 15 minutes
  7. Finance posts invoice in NetSuite
  8. Invoice details visible in Salesforce — sales can follow up on payment

Results

  • Eliminated manual data entry — 80%+ reduction in duplicate data entry across systems
  • Accelerated order fulfillment — Orders reach warehouse in minutes instead of hours
  • Real-time visibility for sales teams — No more calls to operations for status updates
  • Consistent customer data — Single source of truth across CRM and ERP
  • Reduced order errors — No transcription mistakes from manual re-entry
  • Audit-ready compliance — Complete trail of all data synchronization

Integration Flows

Five core integration flows connect the platforms:

1 Customer Sync (Bi-directional) — Keeps Account and Customer records aligned
2 Product Sync — NetSuite Items available in Salesforce for quoting
3 Order Creation — Opportunities become Sales Orders on close
4 Fulfillment Sync — Shipping status returned to Salesforce
5 Invoice Sync — Invoice details for collections visibility
Scheduling
Customer and Order flows run in real-time (event-triggered). Fulfillment and Invoice flows run on scheduled intervals.

Setup Checklist

Salesforce Configuration
  • Connected App configured for Celigo access
  • Custom objects for Order Status and Invoice View created
  • External ID fields on Account for NetSuite linkage
  • API user with appropriate permissions
NetSuite Configuration
  • Token-based authentication configured
  • Custom fields for Salesforce linkage
  • Saved searches for integration data extraction
  • Integration role with required permissions
Celigo Configuration
  • Connections established to both platforms
  • Integration flows deployed and enabled
  • Error notification rules configured
  • Dashboard access for operations team

Monitoring Overview

Error Management

When errors occur:

  • Transient errors retry automatically
  • Persistent errors queue for manual review
  • Email notifications alert the support team
  • Dashboard shows error details and resolution options

Review the Celigo dashboard each morning. Check error queue is empty, verify flows are running, and confirm success rates are above 99%.
